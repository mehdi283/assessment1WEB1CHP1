My Favourite Dish Pizza Recipe


One of the most popular and recognized foods in the world is pizza. A light, fluffy dough is prepared,
and several kinds of delicious toppings, including melting mozzarella cheese and glossy tomato sauce,
are added on top. A delicious experience is created by the mix of tastes. Pizza's creamy, juicy texture and
a perfect mixture of cheese, sauce, and dough are the main reasons I love it. Pizza is always a delicious treat,
whether it's prepared at home or from a restaurant.


Pizza's flexibility is one of its greatest features. From the traditional margherita to heavy meat dishes
or vegetarian sweets, it can be customized to satisfy all tastes. The creamy melted cheese connects
everything, and the rich tomato sauce gives an intense flavor that is enhanced by the crunchy but also chewy crust.
Every pizza is made differently  by the toppings, which give it individuality. This is a pizza for anyone,
no matter your liking for fresh vegetables, hot pepperoni, or a glaze of olive oil.



Ingredients



  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • One packet of fresh dry yeast (2 ¼ tablespoons)

  • One teaspoon of sugar

  • One teaspoon of salt

  • Two teaspoons of olive oil

  • One cup of warm water

  • Half a cup of pizza sauce

  • 1½ cups of mozzarella cheese, shredded

  • Your Favourite garnishes (mushrooms, bell peppers, chicken pepperoni, etc.)



Method



  1. Set the oven temperature to 470°F (250°C).

  2. Dissolve the sugar and yeast in warm water in a bowl. Give it five minutes to rest until it foams.

  3. Add the olive oil, flour, and salt. Mix the dough until it's smooth, around 5 minutes.

  4. For half an hour, cover and let the dough to rise.

  5. Place the dough on a baking sheet that has been oiled after rolling it out into a circle.

  6. spread pizza sauce over the dough.

  7. Add the toppings of your choice after the mozzarella cheese has been prickled.

  8. The crust should be brown and the cheese should be bubbling after 12 to 15 minutes of baking.

  9. After a few minutes of cooling down, cut it into slices and taste it!
